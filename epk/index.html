ABOUT
Blending psychedelic rock, trip hop and desert blues, Atomic Fruit defies the divide between rock and electronic music.

With effects-laden guitars, dreamy vocals and mesmerizing grooves, the Berlin based art rock group takes the listener on a journey through different soundscapes as their songs develop and evolve. Marked by ominous harmonies and suggestive lyrics, their music is a response to the modern world — our attempts to control and understand it, the ambitions that quickly become our undoing, and the life-affirming power of transcendental experiences.

Atomic Fruit is currently recording their debut album to be released in 2023.
